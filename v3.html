INTERIOR PALETTES

Take your pick from an exclusive range of interiors curated by Britain’s renowned designer, Kelly Hoppen MBE. In collaboration with yoo, Hoppen brings a harmonious fusion of simplicity, luxurious textures, and refined finishes. Three distinctive interior styles are available, each showcasing her signature aesthetic of clean lines, neutral tones, and a seamless blend of understated elegance and timeless opulence.

  • Urban
  • See Breeze
  • Vintage

The Urban Palette features taupe stone, dark timber flooring, and exquisite plaster finishes with rich textiles, creating interiors rooted in minimalism and timeless sophistication. Accentuated by thoughtfully curated feature lighting, this fine-tuned design approach infuses elegance into every detail. Kelly Hoppen’s signature touch is evident in the muted linear tones, repeated motifs, and bespoke elements that seamlessly reflect yoo's distinctive panache.

The Sea Breeze style infuses interiors with a refreshing vitality, creating an atmosphere of effortless comfort. This palette blends bleached woods, crisp linen textures, white lacquered oak, and taupe-hued glass to craft a light and inviting ambiance. Textured mirrors and polished nickel ironmongery, paired with clear-cut glass accents, elevate the design with understated elegance. Synonymous with simplicity, the Sea Breeze Palette brings a timeless sense of freshness that resonates throughout the day.

The Vintage Palette embodies a seamless blend of modern luxury and timeless charm. Reclaimed timber paired with black granite stone creates striking floor runners, while rich marble and silk textures evoke an air of refined opulence. Patterned and textured stone, complemented by taupe-hued glass and bronze finishes, adds a touch of sophistication and understated elegance. With its warm, evocative character, the Vintage Palette interiors are imbued with a sense of personal connection.

About Panchshil

Panchshil Realty has been leading the real estate mixed-use luxury developer since 2002. Renowned for its iconic and award-winning real estate development, Panchshil has successfully delivered over 31.7 million sq. ft. of prime real estate, with 27.6 million sq. ft. under development across multi- asset classes in India, Sri Lanka, and the Maldives. Panchshil has played a significant role in putting Pune on the world map and added to its growing global identity through its portfolio that includes high-end residential developments, IT parks, built- to-suit office spaces, and special economic zones (SEZ), hotels, convention centres, luxury retail malls, and landmark hospitality developments. A recipient of multiple awards in luxury homes and office space development, its portfolio of architecturally significant properties, from innovative ground-up developments to revitalized landmarks that define skylines in India includes yoopune by Philippe Starck; Trump Towers Pune, EON Free Zone, Kharadi; the International Convention Centre, South Asia’s largest trade and convention centre, and World Trade Center Pune.
